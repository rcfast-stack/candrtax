Card is the base elevated-surface wrapper — white fill, subtle border, soft shadow, 16px radius.

```jsx
<Card>
  <h3>Individual Income Tax</h3>
  <p>Full-service prep for W-2, 1099, and mixed-income filers.</p>
</Card>
```

Compose with `ServiceCard`/`ContactCard` for domain-specific content rather than restyling Card directly.
